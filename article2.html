Upcoming Concerts and Performances 2024

As the summer winds down and autumn approaches, music enthusiasts have a lot to look forward to with a lineup of exciting concerts from some of the world's top artists. Here are the details on where and when you can catch performances by Ed Sheeran, Billie Eilish, and Bruno Mars.



Ed Sheeran in Bucharest, Romania

• Date: 24 August 2024

• Venue: Arena Națională (National Arena), Bucharest


Ed Sheeran is set to bring his captivating performance to Bucharest on August 24th. Known for his heartfelt lyrics and soulful melodies, Ed Sheeran has become a global sensation. The Arena Națională, with its vast capacity and excellent acoustics, will provide the perfect setting for fans to experience Sheeran's hits like "Shape of You," "Perfect," and "Bad Habits" live. This concert promises to be an unforgettable evening filled with music and emotion.

Billie Eilish in Québec, QC, Canada

• Date: 29 September 2024

• Venue: Centre Videotron, Québec


Billie Eilish, the young and incredibly talented singer-songwriter, will be performing at Centre Videotron in Québec on September 29th. Eilish's unique sound and compelling stage presence have garnered her a massive following worldwide. Fans in Québec will get the chance to see her perform tracks from her critically acclaimed albums, including hits like "Bad Guy," "When the Party's Over," and "Everything I Wanted." The Centre Videotron's modern facilities will ensure a spectacular audio-visual experience for all attendees.

Bruno Mars in Las Vegas, NV, US

• Date: 20 August 2024

• Venue: Dolby Live at Park MGM, Las Vegas


Bruno Mars, known for his high-energy performances and a string of chart-topping hits, will light up Las Vegas on August 20th. Performing at the Dolby Live at Park MGM, Mars will bring his electrifying showmanship to the entertainment capital of the world. Concert-goers can expect to dance the night away to hits like "Uptown Funk," "24K Magic," and "Just the Way You Are." The state-of-the-art Dolby Live venue will enhance the concert experience with its top-notch acoustics and vibrant atmosphere.
