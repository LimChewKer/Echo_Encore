LGBT Anthems

Ever since the pandemic, the presence of LGBT on the internet and the amount of people to resonate and relate with the community has increased infinitely. Music being a huge part of the community has a few undeniably iconic songs that may or may not resonate with your experiences within the community.



1. Born This Way by Lady Gaga

“Born this way” is unarguably a song that has the power to strengthen a person’s sense of uniqueness and self-acceptance. Ever since its release in 2011, it has risen and emphasised the message for people to accept that they are just as perfect as they are, embracing love regardless of race, sexuality, or gender.


2. I’m Coming Out by Diana Ross

Through its addictive beats and empowering lyrics, it has encouraged many to fully embrace and celebrate their own identity, slowly becoming the anthem for the community. Till this day, its impact on the overall music industry and community has been ever-long lasting and remains to be the icon when mentioned.


3. Vogue by Madonna

By the queer icon herself, this dance-pop track has become the cultural phenomenon within the community ever since its release in 1990. The song invites people to step into the spotlight and express themselves freely, quickly becoming the song people relate to within the community.
